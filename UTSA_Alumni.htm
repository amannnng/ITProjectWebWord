About UTSA

Business Alumni, we want to hear from you! The College of Business has created the Business Alumni Network to better serve the needs of business alumni.

 

The College of Business is home to more than 33,000 alumni. And, 86% of business alumni reside within the state ofTexas.

Business alumni are encouraged to reconnect with the College of Business. Subscribe to receive our alumni e-mail newsletter, update your profile information and attend one of the many programs sponsored by the college to see these changes for yourself.

With over 5,700 students, the College of Business has received numerous accolades including the recent ranking as the one of the Top 5 undergraduate business schools in Texas by Bloomberg Businessweek and being named the No. 10 graduate business school in the nation for Hispanics by Hispanic Business magazine. For the tenth straight year the Princeton Review has named us one of the top 10 MBA programs in the nation.
